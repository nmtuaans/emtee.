SEA-TEA PIECE

YEAR: 2023 | MOTION GRAPHIC | 2D | SOUND

"SEA-TEA PIECE" is a 2d motion graphic piece interpret a poem by Yoko Ono in 1961: "Walk over the city, with an empty baby carrier.". From my POV, the poem is about getting out of your comfort zone. It requires a process and I want to focus on the process of realizing how small your comfort zone is and getting out of it.
